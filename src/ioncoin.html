ION

ION is a financial instrument developed on behalf of human wellbeing, nature and beyond.

About

The Works Research Institute offers a token called ION. ION supports R&D of The Works Research Institute, including the development of products for the benefit of human wellbeing and nature, while also sharing financial surplus with ION holders. ION tokens are distributed through a network of agents who can provide information about the token and the products. IONs can be stored in a digital eWallet within the ION app, which also offers updates on product development and market progress.
